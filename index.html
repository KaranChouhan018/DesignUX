Hackathon Timeline

  • 20th July, 2023

    Day 1

    Introduction to the hackathon, its theme, and objectives. Guidelines for participants, including eligibility criteria, team formation, and registration process. Announcement of important dates, such as the registration deadline and hackathon duration.

  • 21th July, 2023

    Day 2

    Participants form teams or join existing ones. Encourage team members to brainstorm and generate innovative UI/UX ideas based on the hackathon theme. Provide resources and inspiration for idea generation, such as design examples or case studies.

  • 22th July, 2023

    Day 3

    Participants conduct research on the target audience, user needs, and current UI/UX trends. Define project goals, scope, and requirements. Create a project plan that outlines the tasks, milestones, and responsibilities.

  • 23th July, 2023

    Day 4

    Teams start designing the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) for their projects. Utilize wireframing tools, design software, or prototyping tools to create visual representations of the intended UI/UX. Iterate on designs, gather feedback, and refine the prototypes based on user-centered design principles.

  • 24th July, 2023

    Day 5

    Participants begin implementing their UI/UX designs into functional prototypes or minimum viable products (MVPs). Develop and integrate necessary features, functionality, and interactions. Collaborate with developers, if applicable, to ensure seamless integration of design and development.
